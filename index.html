Our Story

A Parisian
Corner in
Bangalore

Café Noir brings the soul of a Parisian bistro to the heart of Indiranagar. We believe in slow mornings, generous plates, and coffee that makes you stay just a little longer.

From our signature weekend breakfasts to late-night wine & desserts, every visit feels like a quiet escape from the city's rush.
